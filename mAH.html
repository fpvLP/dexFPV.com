Who is dexFPV?

Hi, my human name is Andres Espinosa, but all my friends call me Dex. I was born in Honduras (Central America), but moved to the United States late 2014! My profesional background is in the Marketing field, and I recently graduated from an intense 3 month Web Developer Boot Camp.
So who is dexFPV?  dex, is the real me, the pilot, the racer, the competitve analytical guy that is always looking for a faster racing line! Since my early days, I have always been training to achieve that highest level of competition. When I was a kid, it was on a dirt bike, as I got older I transition to road and downhill bicycles, and now, all I can think of is FPV. Flying multirotors is the perfect combination of adrenaline with a lower injury factor. (Always take off your props!).
